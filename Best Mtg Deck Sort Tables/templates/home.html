Best MTG Deck For You

Submit Your Collection Evaluate Your Collection

Best MTG Deck For You allows you to better understand what deck to buy given the cards and the decks already in your collection.
The site is easy to use. Just click onSubmit Your Collection and paste the list of your cards to see how much you should spend to build Standard, Modern, Legacy and Pauper Best Decks. Only mkm price trends are used.

Export MTGA Collection: Click hereto download Orens MTGA-EasyExporterV0.1.

Open your Downloads folder and run OrensMTGA-EasyExporterV0.1.exe. If you receive a warning message, click "Run anyway". Do not copy anything while the program is running. A black tab will open and show you the progress. After one minute, it will finish and create a new file named MTGAcollection.txt. Now, click on Submit Your Collection and paste.
